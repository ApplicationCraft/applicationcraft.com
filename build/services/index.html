Why Application Craft?

The best way to explain our expertise is to take a look at the Application Craft and Codio platforms, both of which are built by our team. These products are substantial, sophisticated and incorporate cutting edge technologies at the front and back end.

This expertise allows us to apply the knowledge and insight we have gained from large scale platform development to your application.

We are also experts on HTML5 (hybrid) cross-platform development for mobile devices. While not suitable for every application, there are huge advantages in terms of cost, speed of development and maintenance.

Contact us to discuss how an application development project could improve your business.

What we do

We're here to build your desktop or mobile applications. We specialize in front-end web development (HTML5+CSS+JS) and we often build the back-end, too.

We are very comfortable with all HTML5 based front-end technologies and we use a variety of back end technologies such as Java, Node.js, Ruby and .Net. We are also at home with both relational and NoSQL databases.

We do not do native Mobile App development but we are experts in cross-platform mobile application development, where you want to run on any mobile device (and even desktop) from a single code base. This 'Hybrid' development process also allows deployment to Apple and Google app stores.
