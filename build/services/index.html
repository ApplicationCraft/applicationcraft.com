Developing Your Application: The Process

We follow a clear 3 stage developer process that involves the customer at all stages. Here is a brief summary of what you will experience if you use our services:

Consultation

Stage 1: Free initial consultation

  • We spend 30-60 minutes with you getting a high level overview of your project.
  • We ask you to send us a specification document. The better your specification, the lower the cost will be.
  • We ask you to produce Wireframe images of your applicationâ€™s front-end. Tip: Try using Balsamiq for this.
  • We give you a very approximate project estimate. At this stage it is a simple range and is nothing more than an estimate.
  • At this stage you can decide whether you want to progress with us or not. If you do, then on to the next stage!

Stage 2: Consultation charge before we begin actual development

  • We take your specification and embark on a full analysis of the project. This is where a great spec and comprehensive wireframe mockups will save time and, therefore, cost.
  • Depending on the project size we will take between one and five days to produce our specification with two days being typical.
  • We produce a detailed project breakdown where we detail each component task. All this information is available in our web-based portal so we can explain the project structure and cost in full detail.

Development

Stage 3: The developing begins

  • Once development starts, you can follow our progress in the online Gantt chart within the portal.
  • We deliver updates to you on a continuous basis.
  • All bug tracking is handled through the same portal using a full ticketing system.
  • Testing is a crucial part of the project and one that you are heavily involved in. We schedule testing and associated fixing into the project.
  • Once the project is completed, we hand over all source code to you so you are free to manage the ongoing development yourself if you wish.
