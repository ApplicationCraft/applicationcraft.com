Common Questions

  • Can I cancel my account?

    Absolutely! You can cancel your account at any time, and without any penalties. At the end of your subscription period, you will not be charged further.

  • How many apps can I build using the Application Craft platforms?

    You may build as many as you like with both the free trial and AC Cloud.

  • What can I do / not do with the Free Trial?

    Completely unrestricted access to the AC Cloud platform, with just one exception in that you cannot export/download apps. The Trial also gives you unrestricted access to AC Mobile Build.

  • Are there usage limitations?

    Yes, we have an Acceptable Usage Policy document that covers both how hard your app can hit the platorm as well as restrictions on prohibited content and actions.

  • More questions?

    Get in touch, and we will be happy to answer any questions or queries that you may have.
